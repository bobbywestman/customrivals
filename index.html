Mission
Know some friends, family, or even yourself who might be struggling with physical, emotional, or mental health?
We do. But our struggles don't have to be who we are, we are so much more than that. Sometimes it can be hard to see in out struggles who we really are. Our struggles help us to help others in need, to understand, to listen, to care, to grow and learn because our struggle can't and won't define us if we don't let it.

Do you love working with people and animals and helping others?
Opening our homes to the community, helping families, and saving animals is what we are passionate about. We want to help more and give back. Sometimes the city can get a little overwhelming at times, and it's nice to escape from all of the craziness, to have a place to come together with family and friends, help one another in hard times, to grow, to learn and build each other up, use our skills to better ourselves and others, be free to be our beautiful unique selves.

Who are we?
We might be different, but our love is what keeps us all together. Like any family we might have our struggles, but that doesn't have to stop us, we can let Christ use it to build usup, become stronger in our love for each other, for our community. We don't have to be the same, we can be our unique beautiful selves and make a difference.
